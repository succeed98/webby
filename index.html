WebBank is a national issuer of consumer and small business credit products through Strategic Partner platforms, which include retailers, manufacturers, finance companies, software as a service (“SaaS”) and financial technology (fintech) companies. We are a leading player in the digital lending space, driving innovation in financial products through embedded finance with Strategic Partner platforms.
